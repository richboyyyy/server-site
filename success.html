6 March 2025

1.21 Update

Server expected to update towards end of April or early May.

The 1.21 test server is currently online and can be joined either by typing /join test while already connected, or with test.2b2t.org.

The test server is on a copy of the original world, expected to last for a few days or less and proceed to update within this month.

The following experimental changes are active on the test server, with a high chance of being further tested on the original world with the 1.21 update

  • The Ender Dragon will have a small chance to drop the Eye Armor Trim.
  • Phantoms will always spawn in the Overworld for all players, though in a different way compared to vanilla and rarely. They will always be aggressive.
  • Phantoms will always spawn in the End. They will not be aggressive towards players unless attacked first, which will make all nearby phantoms aggressive.
