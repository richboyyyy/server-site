Welcome to the official shop and thank you for visiting.

About

Our server is a Minecraft server with the goal to never reset the world in a free for all no rules PvP environment, with some modification to the vanilla survival gamemode.

The world is 14 years 3 months old, with a size of over 8200 GBs and over 948,985 players visiting at least once. The IP to connect is yourserver.org.

After many years of development effort, the server is now on the latest Minecraft version with support for hundreds of players online at vanilla-like singleplayer gameplay conditions. Thank you for your support!
