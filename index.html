About

Frog is a Minecraft server with the goal to never reset the world in a free-for-all no-rules PvP environment, with little modification to the vanilla survival gamemode.

The server is 12 years old, with a size of over 50,000 GBs and over 500,000 players visiting at least once. The IP to connect is frog.org.
